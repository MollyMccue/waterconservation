Contributing to Water Conservation is crucial if you desire change. There are numerous modifications and alterations one may make to their daily routine to aid in water saving.

Positive Contributions

Here are some Positive Contributions to make:

  • Turning off running water while doing the dishes, brushing teeth, and etc.
  • Examine the house for leaks
  • Take shorter showers
  • Check the quality of water
  • Only washing clothes when there is a full load

Negative Contributions

There are many Negative Contributions to avoid doing:

  • Flushing the toilet with trash
  • Having the faucet run
  • Not fixing leaks
  • Watering lawns every day
  • Not recycling water
  • Not reusing rain water
